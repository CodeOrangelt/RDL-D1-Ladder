What is our goal?
Project D is the idea for community cooperation. It's an idea, a movement dedicated to prolonging and preserving the community. We want new innovations, new versions, new players, and new modern takes to the game. The idea is that if we can get enough people motivated to create content, drive, and buzz for the game we can bring this game back to it's former glory! Project D doesn't seek admiration, or credit, it's simply the foundation for a movement from a group of like-minded gamers.
Ok, so how can I contribute?
That's a great question! Project D's aim is to house and boost content or innovations anyone from the descent community has. New map? We'll post it! Making D1 Videos? We'll push your channel! We want to be the hub for EVERYTHING descent, so if you love the game and want to do more, you have a home on our website!
