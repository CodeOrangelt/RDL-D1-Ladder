Cheating Policy

Because the competitive nature of a 1v1 Ladder, alongside trackers, and demo/streams, there are a few precautions that may take place to prevent cheating. Do know, that the majority of this community is familiar with the common methods of cheating and rarely go unnoticed by seasoned veterans.

Solo Match Rules

1v1 matches are the primary format. Players must follow these guidelines: - Best of 3 matches - No modifications allowed - Standard weapon loadout only

Duo Match Format

2v2 matches follow a different structure: - Team coordination required - Shared powerup rules - Special scoring system

Capture The Flag

CTF matches have unique requirements: - Flag capture rules - Team size requirements - Base defense guidelines
