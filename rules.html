RDL Expectations, and Rules

Cheating

Because the competitive nature of a 1v1 Ladder, alongside trackers, and demo/streams, there are a few precautions that may take place to prevent cheating. Do know, that the majority of this community is familiar with the common methods of cheating and rarely go unnoticed by seasoned veterans. You will eventually be caught. Cheating warrants an admin-based discretion response, but expect nothing less than a ban. Also, amenable to all participants.

Descent Version

While you play on this Ladder, it will primarily use the port Descent Redux, due to the current development, modern features it offers in contrast to older ports like Rebirth, and Retro. The use of other versions will not be tolerated unless approved by admins. The file size of your .EXE should ALWAYS match your opponents.
